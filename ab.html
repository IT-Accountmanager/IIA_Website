ABOUT US

Established in 2019 with a Mission of bringing Employability Enhancement Education to India at affordable prices, IIA has been Automating lives ever since.

IIA is recognized as a prominent "Finishing School" for engineering aspirants and working employees,aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge gained in colleges and practical application in the industry. To enhance the competency skills of graduating engineers and working corporate, IIA crafted Industrial Oriented Training Programs, which are absolutely relevant to respective industries and their by giving an edge to the students for leverage.


CORE COMITMENTS

To empower individuals with the Automation knowledge and skills to drive innovation for shaping of the future of industries.


To bring Employability Enhancement Education at affordable prices to every individual in the country.
