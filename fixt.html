FIXTURE DESIGN

Fixture design is the specialized art and science of creating precision tools that securely hold and position workpieces during manufacturing processes. Using tools like CATIA, designers craft detailed and optimized fixtures to provide a stable environment for workpieces, facilitating precise and consistent manufacturing. Fixture design is an essential component of modern production, enhancing quality, safety, and overall efficiency in manufacturing operations.
