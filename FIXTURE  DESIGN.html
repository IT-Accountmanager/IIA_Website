FIXTURE DESIGN
90 HOURS




Fixtures play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient manufacturing processes, and
this course aims to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in this field.


Throughout the program, our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of fixture design, including principles of engineering, ergonomic considerations, and dimensional accuracy. You will learn about the different types of fixtures,such as welding fixtures,assembly fixtures,checking fixtures,and locating fixtures,each serving specific purposes in manufacturing process.


Through hands-on exercises and practical demonstrations, you will have the opportunity to explore each type of fixture and understand its unique characteristics and applications. You will develop proficiency in using CAD software to create detailed designs,considering factors like component tolerances, material selection, and ease of use.



Moreover, we will delve into the importance of customization and adaptability in fixture design,enabling you to design fixtures that can accommodate different product variations and production scenarios. We will also explore the integration of automation and robotics in fixture design to further enhance productivity and flexibility.



Join us in this engaging course and embark on a journey to become a skilled fixture design professional, ready to tackle the challenges of the manufacturing industry with confidence and expertise. Prepare to unlock opportunities and make a significant impact through innovative and efficient fixture designs.
