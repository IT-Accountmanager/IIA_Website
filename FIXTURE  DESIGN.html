FIXTURE DESIGN
90 HOURS




Fixtures play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient manufacturing processes, and this course aims to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in this field.


Throughout the program, our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of fixture design, including principles of engineering, ergonomic considerations, and dimensional accuracy. You will learn about the different types of fixtures,such as welding fixtures,assembly fixtures,checking fixtures,and locating fixtures,each serving specific purposes in manufacturing process.
